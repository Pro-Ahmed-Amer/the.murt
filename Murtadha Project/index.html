About

The School Of Life is a non-profit organization that aims to empower people to live intentional and fulfilling lives. We do this by providing educational opportunities that help us learn all the things we need in life but never learned in a traditional school.

Mission

01

To empower people to lead intentional and fulfillinf lives

 02

Our work will be done when people are living fulfilled purposeful lives,reaching their potential, pursuing thier dreams and not being held back by lack of resources (such as access to money, education, mentorship, and employment).

 03

Provide learning opportunities.

Values

01

Paying it forward

 02

Learning by doing/experiential learning

03

Taking ownership/being a leader
